What to know about De BAKERS INN

De BAKERS INN is a bakery situated in the Ashanti region of Ghana. We are dedicated to putting smiles on the faces of our customers through our incredibly tasty products ranging from cakes, pastries to drinks. Our products are special because we bake and make them with you in mind. We bake your dreams into reality. We also do nationwide deliveries. Make your events special with our range of cakes, pastries and drinks. We remain your reliable cakes and pastries partner.

What we offer

Our products include the following:

Cakes:
  • Wedding cakes
  • Birthday cakes
  • Cupcakes
  • Pancakes
Pastries:
  • Doughnuts
  • Meat pie
  • Spring rolls
  • Chips
  • Chinchin and more
Drinks:
  • Fruit juice
  • Sobolo
